Archive Classifications

Classification Meaning
Studio Record A known recorded version exists and is part of the main Warehouse Wrecks archive.
Live-Only Record A song known through live performance footage, with no confirmed studio version currently found.
Character Theme A song primarily centered around one character or department.
Incident Record A song documenting a specific warehouse event, disaster, or operational collapse.

Archive Notes

Some songs directly name characters, while others establish characters through narration, point of view, or repeated references across the Warehouse Wrecks universe.
