Comic Chapter Archive

This section keeps the comic side of Warehouse Wrecks in one place. The songs tell the stories, but the chapters show the damage: Howard's label empire, Kyle's planning chaos, Mónica's Mexico warehouse file, and the fire drill panic around Junior and Nap.

Each chapter is connected to songs, characters, and incidents from the main archive. Some chapters follow a song directly. Others fill the gaps between the jokes, the warehouse rumors, and the parts nobody wrote down when they happened.
